How to Play Othello Five

In Othello Five, gameplay is intuitive and engaging. Click on valid spaces to place your disc on the Othello Five board. A valid move in Othello Five must capture at least one of your opponent's discs by flanking them between your newly placed disc and another of your discs already on the board. The Othello Five match ends when the board is full or when neither player can make a valid move.

  • Mouse Click: Place your disc on a valid space in Othello Five
  • Valid Moves: Othello Five highlights spaces where you can place your disc
  • Capturing: In Othello Five, place discs to trap opponent pieces between yours
  • Winning: Have the most discs of your color when the Othello Five game ends

Othello Five Pro Tips

  • In Othello Five, control the corners and edges of the board whenever possible
  • Othello Five rewards planning - think several moves ahead to set up strategic traps
  • When playing Othello Five, sometimes making a move that flips fewer discs can be more strategic
  • For Othello Five success, focus on board position in early game rather than maximizing captures
