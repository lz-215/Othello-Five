About the Game

Othello Five brings the classic board game strategy to life with modern graphics and engaging gameplay. This timeless game of strategy challenges players to outthink their opponents by strategically placing pieces to flip and capture their opponent's discs. With its perfect balance of simplicity and depth, Othello Five offers endless entertainment for strategy enthusiasts of all skill levels.

How to Play

Click on valid spaces to place your disc. A valid move must capture at least one of your opponent's discs by flanking them between your newly placed disc and another of your discs already on the board. The game ends when the board is full or when neither player can make a valid move.

  • Mouse Click: Place your disc on a valid space
  • Valid Moves: Highlighted spaces show where you can place your disc
  • Capturing: Place discs to trap opponent pieces between yours
  • Winning: Have the most discs of your color when the game ends

Pro Tips

  • Control the corners and edges of the board whenever possible
  • Think several moves ahead to set up strategic traps for your opponent
  • Sometimes making a move that flips fewer discs can be more strategic
  • Focus on board position in early game rather than maximizing captures
